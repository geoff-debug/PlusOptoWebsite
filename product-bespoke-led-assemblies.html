Bespoke LED Assemblies

Bespoke LED lighting design service from concept analysis to production for POS, vending and retail applications.

Overview

To meet customers' increasing demand to adopt LED technology we offer a bespoke design service to develop LED Modules and Light Engines for specific applications. Applications include Industrial & Commercial lighting, point-of-sale and retail lighting.

Design Services:

- Concept analysis

- PCB and circuit design

- Prototyping service

- Tooling

- Samples and pre-production

- Connection solutions

- Drive & control solutions

Application Areas:

- Retail and Point of Sale illumination

- Vending Signage

- Backlighting & Light boxes

- Shelf lighting

- Signage

- Commercial lighting

- Industrial lighting

Technical Facilities:

- LED intensity & spectral testing

- Ageing and life testing

- Thermal design

- Burn in

- LED selection

- Controller software

- Evaluation boards & demo kits
