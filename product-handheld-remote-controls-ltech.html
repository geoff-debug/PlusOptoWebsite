Handheld Remote LED Controls

Designed to communicate with our range of receiver drivers we offer a choice of handheld remote controllers to control LED lighting product. Control options include: Single and Multichannel output for single colour, CT, RGB, RGBW and RGB+CT interfacing with RF, Bluetooth, WiFi and Zigbee receiver drivers.
