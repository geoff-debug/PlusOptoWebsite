Flexible Multi-colour COB LED Strips

COB (Chip-on-Board) LED strips provide a smooth, dot-free linear light source ideal for applications requiring uniform illumination without visible LED hotspots. Available in mono-colour and tuneable white options with a choice of IP ratings.
