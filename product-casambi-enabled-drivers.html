Casambi Enabled LED Drivers

Bluetooth Low Energy wireless lighting control system with drivers, sensors, switches and modules.

Overview

Casambi is a fast growing wireless control system designed for professional lighting control based on the Bluetooth Low Energy protocol, allowing enabled products to communicate. This smart lighting solution allows mesh communication from virtually any mobile device, facilitating flexibility for remote network operation and control of connected LED lighting products.

Our Casambi range includes:

- Casambi Enabled 20W and 36W CC Drivers (Xitanium): 220-240VAC, adjustable output current via simple set

- Casambi Enabled 13W and 40W CC Drivers (LF-ACB series): Compact packages for LED panels and downlights, Zhaga Book13 compliant

- Casambi Enabled 38W CC Driver (127630): Adjustable output 300-1050mA with mains push dimming

- Casambi Enabled 62W CC Driver (127645): Adjustable output 350-1200mA with NTC sensor input

- Casambi Enabled 100W/150W CV (SCL series): Slimline linear drivers, 12V/24V/48V output, up to 93% efficiency

- CBU-TED-LR Wireless Module: Trailing-edge dimmer for incandescent and dimmable LED lamps, 100W max

- CBU-PWM-4-LR 4-Channel PWM: 12-24VDC wireless module for RGBW and colour temperature controls

- Xpress-LR Wireless Smart Switch: Battery operated 8-button control panel, up to 250 nodes

- 127637 0/1-10V RGBW Interface: 4-channel wireless to 0/1-10V converter

- Casambi SFA Switches: Wireless wall switches, no batteries required, choice of finishes

- Casambi CBU-CEFL PIR: Flush mounting PIR sensor, 73/75mm cutout

- Casambi CBU-CESR PIR: Surface mounting PIR sensor
