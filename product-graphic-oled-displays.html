Graphic OLED Displays

Our graphic OLED display are available in three mechanical structures, COB, COG and COG+PCB passive matrix technology. Available graphic monochrome OLED resolutions are in 50x16, 64x128, 100x8, 100x16, 100x32, 200x16, 128x32, 128x64, 128x128, and 256x64. These graphic OLED displays have on-board controllers and interfaces include parallel, serial, I2C and SPI OLED. These OLED display modules have drive voltages of 3V, or 5V depending on resolution/color.

64x32 Micro OLED Display

WINSTAR's OLED WEO006432A is a 0.49-inch micro OLED display module with a resolution of 64x32 dots. The WEO006432A has module dimensions of 14.5×11.6×1.26 mm and an active area size of 11.18×5.58 mm (Diagonal: 0.49"). The micro OLED display is equipped with a built-in SSD1306 IC, supporting the I2C interface. The module operates with a logic voltage of 3V and a duty cycle of 1/32. Additionally, the module supports an internal charge pump supply and external VCC supply. The OLED micro display, also referred to as a micro OLED screen, boasts a high contrast ratio of 10,000:1.
