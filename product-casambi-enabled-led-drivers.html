Casambi Enabled LED Drivers

Our range of Casambi Enabled LED drivers use the Casambi wireless mesh protocol for flexible, intelligent lighting control. Casambi offers easy commissioning via Bluetooth and is compatible with a wide range of sensors, switches and building management systems.

Signify Casambi Enabled LED Drivers 20W/36W

The Philips Xitanium LED drivers are available in 20W and 36W and are Casambi enabled and designed for use with LED spotlights and downlights.

Casambi Enabled 13W and 40W CC Drivers

The Lifud LF-ACB series Casambi enabled LED drivers are available in 13W and 40W with adjustable output current by DIP switch. The compact packages are suitable for LED panels and downlights Class I/II light fixtures.

Casambi Enabled 62W CC Driver

The TCI 127645 62W Constant current Casambi LED driver has adjustable output current from 350mA to 1200mA set by DIP switch.
