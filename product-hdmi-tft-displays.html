TFT Displays with HDMI Interface

Winstar HDMI TFT display modules range from 3.9" to 10.4" and offer plug-and-play compatibility with Raspberry Pi, single-board computers and embedded systems. With standard HDMI connectivity, these displays require no custom driver development, making them ideal for rapid prototyping, digital signage, industrial HMI and educational platforms.

HDMI TFT Displays — Full Range

Winstar's HDMI TFT range covers sizes from 3.9" to 10.4", all featuring a standard HDMI input for simple connection to Raspberry Pi, BeagleBone, Jetson Nano and other single-board computers without the need for custom drivers. USB touch control is supported on touch-enabled models. The range is suitable for embedded systems, digital signage, industrial HMI panels, educational kits and rapid prototyping.

3.9" HDMI TFT 480x128 with PCAP

WF39DTLFSDHG0 is a 3.9" bar-shaped HDMI TFT display. It features a projected capacitive touch panel (PCAP) with 5-point multi-touch. The module boasts a resolution of 480x128 pixels, a brightness of 400 cd/m², a contrast ratio of 500:1, 6 o'clock viewing direction (looking up), 12 o'clock grayscale reversed direction, and a glare glass surface. WF39DTLFSDHG0 adopts TFP401 controller IC, supporting HDMI interfaces (DVI signal) compatible with Raspberry Pi.

4.3" HDMI TFT 480x272 with PCAP

The WF43WSYFEDHGV is a high-performance 4.3" HDMI TFT LCD module with a capacitive touch panel, designed for seamless integration with devices such as Raspberry Pi and other embedded systems. This advanced display supports HDMI signal input (DVI only) and delivers exceptional visual clarity, offering a resolution of 480x272 pixels and a brightness of 800 nits, ensuring crisp and clear images across various applications.

7.0" HDMI TFT Display

The 7.0-inch HDMI TFT display module provides an 800x480 pixel resolution with 500 cd/m² brightness and a wide operating temperature range. HDMI Interface Controller Board

The WWHDMI-00 is a dedicated HDMI interface controller board designed to work with Winstar TFT display modules. It converts HDMI signal to the RGB interface required by the display panel, enabling straightforward connection to Raspberry Pi and other HDMI-equipped single-board computers. This eliminates the need for custom firmware and simplifies the display integration process for engineers and developers.
