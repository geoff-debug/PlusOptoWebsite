IPS TFT Displays

Great Image Quality TFTs from Winstar Display (IPS) In Plane Switching technology means the display offers significant improvements in TFT panels of the light transmission, high quality colour reproduction and a super wide viewing angle. We offer a full range of modules; available sizes from mini IPS TFT of 0.96" to 15.6 inch IPS display, to deliver better performance in a range of demanding situations. We also offer higher brightness version (750~1100 cd/m2) as options for some models and resistive touchscreen (RTP) or capacitive touchscreen (PCAP). These wider view TFT IPS LCD displays can be operated from -20℃ to +70℃ or up to +85℃ with storage temperatures from -30℃ to +85℃ or +90℃.

Infrared Emitters

WINSTAR's IPS TFT LCD modules harness the power of IPS panel (In-Plane Switching) technology, renowned for its faster response time, higher frequency transmittance, and superior color performance. Its most significant advantage lies in precise imaging with wider viewing angles, ensuring consistent color reproduction and high-quality images from various viewing positions. These displays are well-suited for a broad spectrum of applications. These IPS displays boast outstanding performance and versatile characteristics, making them indispensable across diverse sectors such as industrial controls, medical devices, automotive displays, smart home systems, and beyond.
