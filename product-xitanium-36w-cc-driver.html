Xitanium 36W CC Driver

Xitanium Mini wireless LED driver with Zigbee, settable 150-1050mA output for connected lighting systems.

Overview

Xitanium Mini wireless LED drivers are designed to operate LED solutions for general lighting applications. They enable simple, cost effective wireless lighting system for energy saving and comfort. Advanced design of the Mini wireless drivers helps to build a simple, scalable and standardized connected lighting with different functionalities from simple wireless dimming to cloud based operations and integration with gateways.

Specifications:

- AC mains input 220-240VAC

- Output voltage range 24-48VDC

- Adjustable output current via simple set

- Settable current range from 150-1050mA

- Compatible with Zigbee wireless protocols

- Dimensions: 97.2 x 43 x 30mm
