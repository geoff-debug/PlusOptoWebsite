16×2 Character LCD Module

Alphanumeric 16-character × 2-line display with HD44780-compatible controller.

Overview

Industry-standard 16×2 character LCD with built-in HD44780-compatible controller.

Available with white, green or amber backlighting. Parallel or I²C interface.

Display Format16 × 2 characters
ControllerHD44780 compatible
