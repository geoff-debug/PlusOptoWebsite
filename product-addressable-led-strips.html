Addressable LED Strips

Pixel addressable LED strips with individually controllable LEDs for dynamic lighting effects.

Overview

Our range of pixel addressable LED strips incorporate individually controllable LEDs with integrated IC drivers. Each LED can be independently addressed for dynamic colour effects, animations and architectural lighting. Compatible with our range of SPI controllers and DMX-SPI converters.
