Circular TFT Displays

Winstar circular TFT LCD display modules range from 1.28" to 4" in diameter, all utilising IPS wide viewing angle technology for vibrant colour and excellent visibility from any direction. Their distinctive round format makes them ideal for smartwatches, wearables, industrial gauges, medical devices, smart home controls and automotive instrument clusters.

Circular TFT Displays — Full Range

Winstar's circular TFT LCD range covers diameters from 1.28" to 4", all with IPS panels for wide viewing angles and rich colour reproduction. Models are available with SPI, RGB and MIPI DSI interfaces and optional capacitive touch (PCAP). The compact round format suits applications where a traditional rectangular display would be impractical, including smartwatches, fitness trackers, industrial gauges, medical monitors and decorative smart home panels.
