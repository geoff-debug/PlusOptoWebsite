Triac Mains CC Dimmable LED Drivers

Our range of Triac Mains constant current LED drivers feature high efficiency and 0-100% flicker free dimming. Compatible with most leading/trailing triac dimmer modules the drivers are easily integrated into LED lighting schemes. Drivers feature short circuit, overload and over temperature protection with auto-recovery.
