Linear LED Light Engines

We offer a wide range of Linear LED systems which are the ideal solution for LED luminaires which traditionally would have been equipped with fluorescent tubes. Our Linear LED systems portfolio consists of two main series from Philips: Fortimo and CertaFlux.
