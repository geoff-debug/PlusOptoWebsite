3.5″ TFT LCD Module — IPS Wide Viewing

320×480 resolution IPS TFT display with SPI/MCU interface, wide operating temperature range

Overview

The 3.5″ IPS TFT LCD module offers a 320×480 pixel resolution in a compact and reliable package. Featuring IPS (In-Plane Switching) technology, it provides wide viewing angles of up to 170° both horizontally and vertically, making it ideal for human-machine interface (HMI) and industrial panel applications.

The module supports both SPI and MCU (8/16-bit parallel) interfaces and ships with a configurable LED backlight driver. Operating temperature range of -20°C to +70°C makes it suitable for demanding environments where standard consumer-grade panels would fail.

All Plus Opto display modules are supplied with full technical documentation, evaluation code examples, and access to our UK-based engineering support team.

Technical Specifications

Display Size 3.5 inches (diagonal)
Resolution 320 × 480 pixels
Display Type IPS TFT LCD
Interface SPI / MCU 8-bit / MCU 16-bit
Colours 262,144 (18-bit colour)
Brightness 350 cd/m² (typical)
Contrast Ratio 800:1 (typical)
Viewing Angle 170° (H) / 170° (V)
Backlight LED, white, adjustable brightness
Operating Temperature -20°C to +70°C
Storage Temperature -30°C to +80°C
Supply Voltage 3.3 V logic / 5 V backlight
Outline Dimensions 55.0 × 91.0 × 3.35 mm
Active Area 43.2 × 72.0 mm
Connector 40-pin FFC (0.5 mm pitch)
Touch Panel Option Resistive (optional)
