Through Hole LEDs

Through-hole discrete LED lamps in 3mm, 5mm and oval formats from standard indicators to high performance emitters.

Overview

Encompassing a wide range of shapes & sizes, our comprehensive range of through-hole discrete LED lamps can be selected from basic low cost standard indicators through to high performance emitters. A choice of wavelengths & luminous intensities can be offered with lens options to maximise optical performance.

Our range includes:

- Standard 3mm & 5mm Through-hole LEDs: Cost effective T1 and T1 3/4 emitters in wavelengths 470nm-660nm with tape & reel for auto insertion  View Standard 3mm & 5mm LEDs →

- High Performance 5mm TH LEDs: AlInGaP and InGaN chip material with superior high-temperature, moisture resistance and anti-UV properties

- Oval LEDs: 4mm and 5mm through hole with wide field of view for information displays, variable message signs & full-colour video screens
