Hand-Held Remote Controls

Hand-held RF remotes with colour wheel and push button control for dimming, CT and RGBW.

Overview

To facilitate control of our range of 2.4GHz RF products we offer a range of hand-held remote controllers. Colour wheel and push button types allow dimming control, CT and RGB, RGBW & RGBWW colour control. Our remotes are designed to pair with our range of RF receiver/drivers for a complete wireless lighting control solution.

Our range includes:

- RF 2.4GHz: Miboxer, single and multi-channel controllers, (DIM/RGB/RGBW/RGB+CCT)  View Details →

- RF 2.4GHz: Ltech single and multi-channel controllers, (DIM/RGB/RGBW/RGB+CCT)  View Details →
