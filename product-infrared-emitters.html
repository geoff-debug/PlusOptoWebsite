Infrared (IR) Emitters

We offer a comprehensive range of infrared emitters and detectors in SMD and through-hole packages from leading manufacturers. The range covers a wide spectrum of wavelengths and output powers for applications including proximity sensing, remote control, object detection, data transmission and illumination.

Low Power Through-hole Infrared Emitters

Surface mount infrared emitters in a compact PLCC package for proximity sensing, object detection and data transmission applications.

Low Power SMD Infrared Emitters

Through-hole infrared emitters in 3mm and 5mm packages offering high radiant intensity for remote control, sensing and illumination applications.

High Power SMD Infrared Emitters

Our range of high power SMD emitters in 3535 package with integral lens for directivity. Specially suited to CCTV and recognition systems the output is in the range of 850 and 940nm. A choice of Beam angles are available.
