Surface Mount LEDs

Comprehensive SMD LED range including Chip LEDs, PLCC, Mid/High Power, COB, RGB, addressable and horticultural types.

Overview

Surface Mount LEDs (SMD) offer a compact form and function with no wires allowing a seamless integration with the PCB. Surface Mount LEDs are broadly categorised into 3 types relating to the power: Low Power, Mid Power and High Power along with Chip-On-Board (COB) types.

Our comprehensive range includes:

- Chip LEDs: 0402 to 1206 outline with top, reverse, and right-angle-mount options  View Chip LEDs →

- Low Power PLCC LEDs: Choice of packages and colours, including White and Multi-colour.  View Details →

- Mid Power LEDs: High efficacy devices in a choice of packages, LM80 standard & ANSI binning for luminaires and linear LED fittings  View Details →

- High Power LEDs: Drive currents up to 3.0A in a choice of packages and colours  View Details →

- Multi-colour LEDs: RGB, RGBW & RGB+CT in single packages  View Multi-colour LEDs →

- UV LEDs: UV-A, B and C emitters for curing, sterilization, purification & sensing applications.  View Details →

- Pixel Addressable LEDs: SPI protocol RGB/RGBW with integrated IC for dynamic lighting schemes  View Details →

- Chip On Board (COB) LEDs: High flux, high efficiency, multi-chip emitters from 4W to 100W  View Details →

- LEDs for Horticulture: Wavelength-optimised emitters for horticultural grow lighting

- Infrared (IR) Devices: IR Emitters & Detectors in a range of packages for illumination and sensing applications.  View Details →
