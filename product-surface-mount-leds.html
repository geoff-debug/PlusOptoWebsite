Surface Mount LEDs

Comprehensive SMD LED range including Chip LEDs, PLCC, Mid/High Power, COB, RGB, addressable and horticultural types.

Overview

Surface Mount LEDs (SMD) offer a compact form and function with no wires allowing a seamless integration with the PCB. Surface Mount LEDs are broadly categorised into 3 types relating to the power: Low Power, Mid Power and High Power along with Chip-On-Board (COB) types.

Our comprehensive range includes:

- Chip LEDs: Miniature SMD LEDs from 0402 to 1206 outline with top, reverse, and right-angle-mount options   View Chip LEDs →

- Low Power PLCC LEDs: Packages including 3014, 3020, 4014, 3528, 2835, 5050 and side emit in mono-colour, White and RGB/W

- Mid Power LEDs: High efficacy devices with LM80 approval and ANSI binning for luminaires and linear LED fittings

- High Power LEDs: Drive currents up to 1.5A in mono-colours, white and 3-chip RGB configuration

- Multi-colour LEDs: RGB, RGBW & RGB+CT in single packages for full-colour emission

- Pixel Addressable LEDs: SPI addressable RGB with integrated IC for decorative and dynamic lighting

- Chip On Board (COB) LEDs: Multi-chip emitters from 4W to 100W with high flux, efficiency and CRI

- LEDs for Horticulture: Wavelength-optimised emitters for horticultural grow lighting

- Touch Sensor LEDs: Non-contact capacitive touch sensing in 5050 SMD package
