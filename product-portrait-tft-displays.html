Portrait TFT Displays

Our portrait TFT LCD display modules range from 0.68" to 8.0" and are designed for vertical orientation applications. Using IPS technology for superior colour accuracy and wide viewing angles, these displays support MIPI DSI and SPI interfaces and are ideal for handheld devices, point-of-sale terminals, medical equipment, vending machines and industrial control panels.

Portrait TFT Displays — Full Range

Winstar's portrait TFT range spans 0.68" to 8.0" with IPS technology throughout, delivering consistent colour and wide viewing angles in a vertical layout. Models are available with SPI, RGB and MIPI DSI interfaces, with optional capacitive touch (PCAP). The portrait format is well suited to handheld devices, vending machines, industrial HMI panels, medical monitoring and point-of-sale terminals.
