UV LEDs

We offer a comprehensive range of UV LEDs spanning UV-A, UV-B and UV-C wavelengths, plus combined UV and COB modules. Applications include curing, sterilization, water purification, phototherapy, bio-analysis and counterfeit detection.

UV-A LEDs

UV-A LEDs in the 315–400nm spectral range offer high optical power and product stability with high thermal conductivity aluminium nitride ceramic packages. Available in 60° or 120° viewing angles for curing, exposure, printing and test equipment applications.

View Details →

UV-B LEDs

UV-B LEDs in the 280-315nm spectral range offer high optical power in ratings from 0.2W to 1.1W. Applications include Sterilisation, Phototherapy, Bio-analysis and Detection.

View Details →

UV-C LEDs

UV-C LEDs in the 200–280nm range offer high stability and thermal conductivity using aluminium nitride ceramic packages. Applications include water and air purification, sterilisation and germicidal disinfection.

View Details →

UV-A and UV-C Combined LEDs

2-chips-in-one package offering dual wavelength emission covering 270–285nm and 395–495nm simultaneously. Available in 3535 package, 0.2W to 0.5W, with 120° emission angle. Suited to blood analysis, food preservation, curing and counterfeit detection.

View Details →

UV-A COB LED Modules

Chip on Board modules providing increased optical power from multiple chips bonded directly onto an MCPCB in high density configuration. Spectral range 365–410nm, up to 300W, thermoelectric design with excellent heat conduction and 60° emission angle. Customisable packaging available.

View Details →

UV-C LED Downlight

The PFD89-UVC-W is designed for air cleaning and lighting in a single downlight, integrating UV-C and white LEDs. Spectral range 260–285nm, 68–77mm cutout diameter, available in various power ratings and white colour temperatures.
