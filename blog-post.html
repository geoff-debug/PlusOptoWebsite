New Signify Linear LED Light Engines Now Available

15 January 2025

Plus Opto is pleased to announce that we are now stocking the latest generation of Signify linear LED light engines, bringing industry-leading luminous efficacy and long-term colour consistency to the commercial lighting market.

What Are Linear LED Light Engines?

Linear LED light engines are high-power LED modules designed to fit into standard luminaire form factors, making it easy to upgrade or build new luminaires with state-of-the-art LED technology. Signify's latest portfolio offers efficacies exceeding 200 lm/W in certain configurations, representing a significant advance over previous generations.

Key Features of the New Signify Range

The new light engines feature several improvements over previous generations:

  • Improved efficacy: Up to 210 lm/W at rated current, significantly reducing energy costs for large-scale installations.
  • Enhanced colour consistency: MacAdam 2-step binning across the full lifetime of the module, guaranteeing visual uniformity across multi-luminaire installations.
  • Wider CCT range: Available in 2700K, 3000K, 3500K, 4000K and 5700K colour temperatures.
  • R9>50 options: High-CRI variants are available for applications where colour rendering is critical, such as retail and hospitality.
  • Extended lifetime: L70 lifetime rated at 100,000 hours under standard operating conditions.

Applications

These light engines are particularly well suited for:

  • Linear suspended and recessed office luminaires
  • Retail display and accent lighting
  • High-bay industrial lighting
  • Horticultural lighting (specialist spectra available)

Driver Compatibility

The Signify linear light engines are designed for use with constant-current LED drivers in the 500 mA to 1400 mA range. They are compatible with DALI 2, 0–10 V and PWM dimming protocols. Plus Opto also stocks a full range of compatible LED drivers, including Tridonic, Inventronics and Meanwell options.

Availability and Ordering

Stock is available from our UK warehouse for immediate despatch. Sample quantities are available for evaluation. For pricing, availability and technical guidance, please contact our sales team or call us on +44 (0) 1942 671122.
