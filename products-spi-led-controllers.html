SPI Controllers

To support our range of pixel addressable digital LED strips we offer a range of SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) LED controllers. Addressable LED pixels allow users to create dynamic lighting effects along linear LED strip and may also be used with our addressable modules. Many controllers have built-in scenes that may be adjusted to produce the desired lighting effect. SPI controllers also have remote control options and types that interface with DMX protocol facilitating larger lighting schemes.
