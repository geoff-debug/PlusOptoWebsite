LED Controllers

We offer a wide choice of LED controllers to support our range of LED assemblies, modules and fittings for use in lighting applications — from basic installations to larger professional, integrated & connected schemes.

Overview

We offer a wide choice of LED controllers and drivers to support our range of LED assemblies, modules and fittings for use in lighting applications. Lighting schemes may be realised from basic installations to larger professional, integrated & connected schemes.

DMX / DMX512 (Digital Multiplex) is an industry standard protocol for digital communication networks commonly used to control lighting and effects. DALI bus-mastering protocol is a well established, industry standard dimming control for commercial lighting installations. WiFi offers wireless control using the 2.4GHz 802.11b/g/n protocol. SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) allows individual control of addressable LEDs. RF 2.4GHz facilitates wireless remote control from wall mounted touch panels and hand-held transmitters. Bluetooth Mesh (BLE) protocol allows communication for connected lighting schemes.
