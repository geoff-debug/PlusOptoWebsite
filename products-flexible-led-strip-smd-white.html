Flexible White LED Strip SMD

From a wide range of LED emitter types we offer white flexible LED strips in a choice of colour temperatures, LED densities and power ratings. For high colour rendering, CRI >90+ can be specified along with tight colour matching using small step SDCM. The choice of packaging includes IP20, IP62, IP65, IP67, IP68.
