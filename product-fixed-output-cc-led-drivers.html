Fixed Output CC LED Drivers

We offer an extensive range of quality Fixed Output Constant Current LED drivers from major manufacturers to facilitate reliable, efficient power management for LED products.

SP10 Series miniature CC LED Drivers

The Snappy SP10 series are miniature 10W constant current LED drivers with fixed output current 350mA, 500mA and 700mA.

View Details →

SE/SDS Series Constant Current LED Drivers

The SE/SDS series of constant current fixed output LED drivers provide a cost effective solution to power requirements for a wide range of LED products. Drivers are rated IP20 for indoor applications and easy installation is facilitated by connection via covered screw terminals.

View Details →

Lifud Series Compact CC LED Drivers

The Lifud series of constant current LED drivers are designed for luminaires and LED Light panels. the drivers are flicker free and available in power ratings ranging from 5W to 60W. Output current is adjustable and is set by DIP switch.

View Details →

Philips Xitanium Linear CC LED Drivers

Philips Sensor-Ready drivers are ideal for use with sensors and building management systems. With the integrated DALI 2.0 power supply it is easy to power sensors and wireless modules directly from the driver. The driver also features integrated energy metering for use in building management systems.
