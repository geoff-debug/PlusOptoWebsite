Bar TFT Displays

Winstar Bar TFT displays are widescreen, bar-type TFT LCD modules designed for applications where a compact, elongated display format is required. With high brightness, wide viewing angles and a range of sizes, they are ideal for industrial HMI panels, digital signage, instrumentation and automotive displays.

3.9" Bar TFT Display

WINSTAR Display 3.9-inch Bar-type color TFT LCD display with a resolution of 480x128 pixels. This 3.9" WF39B TFT-LCD display offers high-quality and colorful images, making it especially suitable for server systems, audio systems, advertising displays, auto/aviation/marine equipment, POP displays, security equipment, industrial equipment, outdoor intercom systems, control panels, medical equipment, and drone controls.

The WFN0460B2S0YADNC000 is a 4.6 inch screen in a bar-type TFT LCD display format with 800x320 pixels resolution.
