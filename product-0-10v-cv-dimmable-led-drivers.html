0-10V CV Dimmable LED Drivers

We offer a wide range of constant voltage analogue dimming LED drivers using the 0-10V dimming protocol. Applying a control voltage of between 0 and 10 volts DC varies the connected LEDs intensity. Power ratings between 10W and 1000W are available.

SL-VFM series 0-10V CV Dimmable Drivers

The Snappy SL-VFM series offer Triac, 0-10V, PWM, Potentiometer dimming in one driver with constant voltage output 12V and 24VDC and power ratings 75W to 150W.

SD-VLP series 0-10V CV dimmable Driver

The Snappy SD-VLP series LED drivers are 0-10V constant current LED drivers available in 12V or 24V with power rating 75W.

View Details →

LM-G1A2 Series 0-10V CV Dimmable LED Drivers 36W to 150W

The Ltech LM-G1A2 series are 5-in-1 constant voltage dimmable LED Drivers featuring 0/1-10V, PWM/RX and Push DIM and are available in 12V and 24VDC output with power ratings from 36W to 150W

LM-G2A2 Series 0-10V CV Dimmable LED Drivers 75W to 100W

LT704-5A Low voltage 0-10V 4-Channel CV LED Driver
